This Satan has a few moves inspired or taken directly from various fighting games, but not too many, because it's Satan.


 Backward Dash :
This is a classic back dash, Satan style, allowing him to move far from the opponent pretty quickly. Satan is invincible (but can be grabbed) during the rolling animation.

Forward Dash :
This is the classic SF-type dash. You also have control during the dash, allowing you to attack during it.

Satan Guard : when hit.
Instead of guarding normally, Satan will show his will to fight and roll away or beg for mercy. But maybe it's a strategy to win the fight ?



All Satan's Basics are Specials because his moves are too awesome to be considered Basics.

 Left Punch :
Satan performs a very powerful left punch that is the beginning of a more powerful combination. Beware, it's deadly!

 Right Punch :
Satan performs a very powerful right punch that is the beginning of a more powerful combination. Beware, it's deadly!

 Multiple Punch (x5) : or
Satan performs a very powerful combination of left and right punches that are so quick the opponent won't see them coming. Can be performed up to 5 times, with any combination of X and Y.

Dynamic Kick :
Satan performs the very special dynamic kick that most probably sent Cell directly to Hell.



Are the Cameras Rolling !? :
Satan performs a lightning speed combo with his mighty punches before kicking the opponent to the face with a Super Dynamic Kick.

Coup de Grace :
After jumping dynamically to kick his opponent and stunning him with his flurry of punches, Satan performs his Surprise punch, aka the deadly technique which allowed him to beat Android 18 during the last Budokai Tournament.



Shun Satan Satsu :
Satan dashes to the opponent and attempts to grab him. If he succeeds, get ready for some awesome Satan Show, involving full screen madness and nice explosions.



 Satan has a special compatibility with Goku Z2, to give more of a fullgame "feeling".

Goku Patch :
You'll find a patch for Goku Z2 in Satan's folder (you can download Goku Z2 on the same website you've downloaded Satan from).
Just extract the archive in Goku's folder to apply the patch.

Infinite Genkidama Boost :
Once the patch has been applied, you can play with Goku, using Satan as partner, and feel the incredible power given by all the inhabitants of the Universe.
Goku will be fighting alone in Team mode, but will have access to Infinite Genkidama thanks to Satan asking everybody in the Universe to lend Goku their energy.
Yeah, that's basically the only moment Satan was really useful in Dragon Ball, and we wanted to show this in the character. :-)





Satan has a few combos in his inexistent arsenal, and this is his main weapon to not get destroyed in less than 5 seconds.

Chaining :
- Dynamite Kick can be combo'd from Multiple Punch at any time.
- Dynamite Kick can be performed in the air.
- Punch Normals can be Super Cancelled.



 Random notes :
- Guard with Satan to avoid danger.
- Satan automatically rolls out of danger when hit.

Configuration file :
In Satan Z2's root folder, there is a config.txt file in which you can set some preferences :
- You can choose to use either solid or transparent FX.
- You can choose which Super Background to use when you kill your opponent with a Super move.
