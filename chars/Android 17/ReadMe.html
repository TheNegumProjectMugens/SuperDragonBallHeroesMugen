Character Information:

Android 17, twin brother of Android 18 is known for being cold and cruel. In Mirai Trunks' time, 17 along with 18 destroyed most of the world. Killing off the entire Z Fighter gang, except for Gohan and Trunks, until much later when the two Androids killed Gohan. It wouldn't be until years after Gohan's death, and after Trunks went back into the past to help defeat Cell, that he would be strong enough to return and finally kill 17 and 18 for good.
In the regular story timeline, Android 17 and 18 are freed by their creator Dr.Gero, who 17 quickly kills. 17 and 18 only want to kill Goku this time around, but after getting side tracked by Vegeta and Piccolo, their journey is ended abrubtly when Cell shows up absorbing 17 and later 18.
Although 18 is freed from Cell, 17 dies along with him. Only to be wished back and later live a seemingly reclusive lifestyle, enjoying recreation time hunting, and no longer with a desire to kill Goku.
17 returns once again to attack your MUGENs with his saucy sister 18. While 18 deals mostly strong ranged attacks, 17s strenth lies in is close range melee style fighting. He has alot of combo power and some stun type moves.

Chains and Fighting Tips:
1. When using the "Fireball Chain" special attack, continue tapping any 'P' button to chain up to 5 fireballs.
2. After performing the "Fireball Chain" command, hold on to the P button instead of tapping it for a super charged, stunning, ki blast!
3. Both "Ki Charge" and "Ki Blast" attacks can be performed in air.
4. 17's "Flying Double Kick" attack can normally only be performed while standing. However, it can be used during air combos as well.
5. 17 is invulnerable during his "Energy Field(Ki shield)" super, use it to evade devastating attacks and damage your foe.
6a. Teleport attack commands:
--Hold Forward+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Back+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Up + Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Down+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously, This one cannot be chain with by other Teleport Attacks.
7. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
8. 17s "Finger Beam" super changes based on what pallette the player selects. See Pallette options, to determine what weapon to choose.
