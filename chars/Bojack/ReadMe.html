Character Information:

Bojack is the leader of an ancient gang of powerful space pirates, imprisoned within a star and bound by the 4 Kai planets long ago. He and his group were freed when Cell blew up King Kai's planet, killing everyone on it including King Kai and Goku. Bojack and his gang were unbound and made their first target Earth.

Bojack and company crashed a special Martial Arts Tournament, defeating the world's strongest one by one until Gohan was eventually beaten. Goku intervened breaking through from the world of the dead into the world of the living, interrupting Bojack's attempt at killing Gohan, and inspiring Gohan to give it everything he had.

Gohan finally transforms into Super Saiyan 2, defeating what remains of Bojack's gang aside from Zangya, who Bojack kills himself(he probably felt DBZ needs to remain a sausage fest, no girls allowed). Gohan and Bojack have one last shoot out, but Gohan's power overwelms Bojack and he's destroyed.

Chains and Fighting Tips:
1. The homing Ki Ball can be bounced away by opponents, which can damage you in return.
2. The finger beam can be charged. Full charge fires 3 beams instead of 1.
3. Teleport attack commands:
--Hold Forward+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Back+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Down+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously, This cannot be chain with by other Teleport Attacks.
4. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
5. Attacks do less damage after each successful hit in a combo. After 10 hits you can only do 20% damage per attack. Supers are not affected by this.
6. Use air dash wisely, excessive use causes energy loss. When out of power you may only use a single air dash before landing.
